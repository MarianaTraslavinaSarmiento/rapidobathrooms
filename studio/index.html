About Rapido Bathrooms

Rapido Bathrooms brings over 15 years of experience renovating high-end, modern bathrooms throughout Washington, DC, including NW, NE, and SE.

Our goal is to complete most bathroom remodels in 10 working days. “Working days” are active construction days during normal work hours. If we’re waiting on inspections, approvals, or other external steps, those days don’t count as working days. We communicate timelines clearly from day one, so expectations stay aligned throughout the project.
