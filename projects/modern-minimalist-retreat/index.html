Modern Vanity Upgrade — Floating Cabinetry, LED Mirror & Clean Finishes

This bathroom remodel update focuses on the details that instantly elevate a space: a sleek floating vanity with rich dark cabinetry, a clean white countertop, and a bright LED backlit mirror framed by a modern hex-tile accent band. The minimalist design keeps the room feeling open and high-end, while the wall-mounted vanity adds visual space and makes cleaning easier. Finished with contemporary hardware and crisp lines, this project is a great example of modern bathroom remodeling, vanity replacement, and bathroom renovation work that delivers a fresh, custom look without overcomplicating the design.
