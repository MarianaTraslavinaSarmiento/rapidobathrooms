Luxury Spa Bathroom Remodel — Calacatta-Style Tile, Black Soaking Tub & Gold Fixtures

This luxury bathroom remodel delivers a bold, modern spa feel with oversized Calacatta-style porcelain tile wrapping the floors and walls for a seamless, high-end finish. A striking matte black freestanding soaking tub becomes the centerpiece, paired with warm brushed-gold fixtures that add contrast and elevate the entire design. The walk-in shower features a clean glass enclosure and premium details for a sleek, open look, while recessed lighting keeps the space bright and polished.
