Charcoal Tile + Floating Vanity Upgrade

Rapido Bathrooms completed a sleek, contemporary bathroom transformation that proves small spaces can feel high-end. The finished design features dramatic large-format charcoal wall tile, a clean floating vanity with a marble-look countertop, a modern vessel sink, and an oversized LED backlit mirror that elevates the entire room.

From Basic & Bright to Bold & Modern

The “before” bathroom had a simple layout and dated finishes that didn’t maximize style or impact. In the “after,” the space feels more refined and architectural—clean lines, darker tones, and modern lighting create a boutique-hotel look without sacrificing function.

Design Details That Make the Room Feel Custom
  • ✓ Large-format charcoal wall tile for a seamless, upscale look with fewer grout lines
  • ✓ Floating vanity + vessel sink for a clean, modern profile and easier floor visibility
  • ✓ Marble-look countertop that adds contrast and a polished finish
  • ✓ LED backlit mirror that improves lighting and gives the vanity wall a premium glow
Built to Last

Our remodels focus on durability as much as aesthetics—proper prep, precise installation, and clean finishing so the bathroom performs as good as it looks.
