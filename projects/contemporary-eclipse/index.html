Luxury Bathroom Remodel in McLean — Backlit Tile, Floating Vanity & Walk-In Shower

This McLean bathroom remodel delivers a sleek, modern spa experience with clean lines, premium finishes, and dramatic lighting throughout. The new layout features a large frameless glass walk-in shower with oversized porcelain wall tile, a recessed shelf detail, and soft LED accent lighting that creates a warm, high-end glow.

A floating double vanity anchors the space with dual vessel sinks, wall-mounted fixtures, and a full-width backlit mirror, while the seamless tile flooring keeps the room feeling open, bright, and easy to maintain. From the custom lighting details to the precision tile installation, this project is a standout example of bathroom remodeling in McLaren, modern bathroom renovation, and luxury shower design built to look incredible and perform for years.
