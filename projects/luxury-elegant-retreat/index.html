Bright White Bathroom Remodel with Black Accents & Subway Tile Shower

Rapido Bathrooms transformed this dated bathroom into a crisp, modern space with a brand-new tiled shower, updated storage, and a clean black-and-white finish that feels timeless. From full demo to final detailing, this remodel focuses on durability (proper prep + waterproofing) and a fresh, airy look that fits everyday living.

What Changed: Full Demo to Fresh Modern Finish

This project started with an older, partially torn-out bath (exposed studs, outdated tile, and subfloor work in progress) and ended with a clean, finished shower and a bright layout that feels brand new. The before-and-after photos show the full rebuild: structure, pan, tile, and final glass.

Centerpiece Upgrade: A Clean, Modern Shower Build

The finished shower features soft gray subway tile walls paired with a white mosaic tile floor and a center drain for a sharp, balanced look. A full-height glass enclosure keeps the space open while giving the shower a sleek, easy-to-clean finish.

  • ✓ Gray subway tile for a modern, neutral tone
  • ✓ White mosaic floor tile for texture + slip-friendly grip
  • ✓ Clear glass enclosure for a bigger, brighter feel
Smart Storage: Open Shelving That Actually Works

Instead of cluttered corners, this remodel added built-in wood shelves for towels and daily essentials—simple, clean storage that looks intentional and keeps the bathroom organized.
