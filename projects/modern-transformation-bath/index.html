Bathroom Remodel en Arlington, Virginia (Northern Virginia)

This Arlington, Virginia bathroom remodel was completed in under 10 working days on-site (additional days were only for inspection scheduling). We removed the existing tub and shower, completed demolition, framing, and rough plumbing/electrical, then rebuilt the layout to create one large his-and-hers shower and a double-sink vanity so each person has their own side of the bathroom. The finished space delivers a clean, spa-style look with marble-look tile floors and walls, a frameless glass shower, a freestanding soaking tub, floating vanities, and matte black fixtures—a high-end upgrade for homeowners searching bathroom remodeling in Arlington VA and Northern Virginia.
