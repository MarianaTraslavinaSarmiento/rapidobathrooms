Airy Spa Bathroom Remodel with Freestanding Tub & Herringbone Shower in Northern Virginia

Rapido Bathrooms transformed this bathroom into a bright, spa-like retreat that feels open, modern, and effortless. The layout highlights a sleek freestanding soaking tub placed by the window for a calm, natural-light moment, while the oversized walk-in shower brings a luxury-hotel vibe with clean lines and premium finishes. A soft palette of whites and light grays keeps everything timeless, and the brushed brass fixtures add warmth and sophistication without overpowering the space.

Tubside Comfort with a Bright, Open Layout

The freestanding tub is positioned to take advantage of natural light, creating a relaxing corner that feels like a private spa. Surrounding white subway tile keeps the room crisp and airy, while the simple curb and open shower entry help the bathroom feel larger and more seamless.

Warm Brass Details + Modern Finishing Touches

Brushed brass hardware adds a refined contrast against the cool tile tones, tying the shower fixtures and controls together for a cohesive look. The pendant light brings personality and a soft glow, while the clean tile transitions and precise installation details give the entire remodel a custom, high-quality finish.
