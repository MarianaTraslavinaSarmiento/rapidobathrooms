Luxury Bathroom Remodel in Vienna Featuring Custom LED Lighting

This high end bathroom remodel in Vienna, Virginia showcases a modern design built around clean lines, premium materials, and custom LED lighting that transforms the entire space.

The layout was designed to feel open, bright, and relaxing, with large-format tile throughout and a seamless walk-in shower. A floating vanity adds a sleek contemporary look, while the freestanding soaking tub becomes a true centerpiece especially when illuminated by the custom LED accents.
