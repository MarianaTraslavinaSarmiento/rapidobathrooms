Modern Gray Walk-In Shower

This sleek bathroom remodel features a clean, modern walk-in shower designed for everyday comfort and easy maintenance. We used large-format gray tile to create a seamless, spa-like look, paired with a bold black shower base for strong contrast and a contemporary finish. The glass enclosure keeps the space open and bright while giving the shower a high-end, custom feel.

Details like a recessed, illuminated niche, rainfall showerhead with hand shower, and a streamlined vanity bring both function and style together. The result is a minimalist, upscale bathroom upgrade that feels refined, durable, and timeless—perfect for homeowners looking for a modern bathroom renovation with premium workmanship.
