Southeast Washington, DC Bathroom Remodel — Luxury Upgrade While Keeping the Existing Glass

This bathroom remodel in Southeast Washington, DC delivered a full modern transformation while honoring one key homeowner request: keep the existing shower glass enclosure. Instead of demolishing and replacing it, our team designed the renovation around the glass with precise measurements, adjusted wall alignment, and custom tile layout so everything fit cleanly and looked intentional.

The finished space features large-format marble-look wall tile, a custom mosaic accent band, a properly sloped tiled shower floor, a built-in niche, and a corner bench, plus a refreshed vanity area with a double vanity and vessel sinks, upgraded mirrors, and updated lighting for a brighter, more custom feel. We also updated the vanity area with a double vanity and vessel sinks, modern mirrors, and upgraded lighting to brighten the room and give it a more custom feel.
