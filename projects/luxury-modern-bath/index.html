Dark Marble-Style Bathroom — Frameless Glass Shower & LED Mirror Upgrade

This modern bathroom remodel delivers a bold, high-end look with dramatic dark marble-style tile installed across the walls and floor for a seamless, luxury finish. The large-format pattern creates a clean, continuous flow that instantly elevates the space, while recessed lighting keeps the room bright and balanced against the darker palette. We paired the statement tile with crisp white fixtures and warm wood accents to keep the design feeling modern, not heavy.

For the shower, we installed a sleek frameless glass panel and upgraded the system with a contemporary rain-style shower head and handheld, creating a spa-like experience while keeping the layout open and functional. The vanity area was refreshed with a floating modern vanity, a clean-lined white sink, and an LED backlit round mirror that adds flattering task lighting and a custom feel. From precise tile alignment to polished finishing details, this project is a great example of quality-driven bathroom remodeling built for style, durability, and everyday comfort.
