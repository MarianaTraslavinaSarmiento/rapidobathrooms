Luxury Marble-Style Bathroom Remodel — Freestanding Tub, Frameless Glass & Matte Black Details

This luxury bathroom remodel delivers a bold, modern spa feel with full-height marble-look porcelain tile that creates a clean, seamless finish from floor to wall. The space features a striking freestanding soaking tub set beneath a large window, plus a custom walk-in shower designed for comfort and everyday performance—perfect for homeowners searching for high-end bathroom remodeling and modern bathroom renovation.

The shower was finished with a frameless glass enclosure and premium hardware, paired with a ceiling-mounted rain shower and a handheld system in matte black for a crisp, contemporary contrast. Built-in niches keep the design functional and clutter-free, while the large-format tile keeps grout lines minimal for a cleaner look and easier maintenance.
