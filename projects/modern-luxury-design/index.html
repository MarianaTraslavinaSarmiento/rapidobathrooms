Walk-In Shower Feature Wall & Oversized Backlit Mirror

This luxury bathroom remodel was designed to feel calm, modern, and custom from the moment you walk in. We installed large-format, stone-look tile throughout the walls and floor to create a seamless, upscale look with minimal grout lines and a clean architectural finish. The centerpiece of the vanity area is an oversized round backlit mirror that adds a warm glow and instantly elevates the space, paired with a sleek floating vanity, a stone-style countertop with an integrated sink, and premium fixtures for a refined, hotel-inspired feel.

For the shower, we built a modern walk-in layout with a privacy wall and a custom niche that includes soft LED accent lighting, creating both function and a high-end design detail. Recessed ceiling lights keep the room bright, while the warm lighting around the mirror and niche adds depth and atmosphere—perfect for a spa-like experience at home. If you’re looking for a modern, detail-driven bathroom renovation with high-quality tile work and custom lighting, this project is a great example of what a true luxury remodel should look like.
