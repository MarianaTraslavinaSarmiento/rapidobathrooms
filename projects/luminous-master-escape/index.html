Skylight Luxury Upgrade

This bathroom was designed to feel modern, open, and high-end from every angle. We created a dramatic focal point with a freestanding soaking tub centered in front of a striking statement slab-style tile feature wall, then balanced it with large-format gray porcelain tile for a clean, contemporary finish.

A frameless glass panel keeps the space airy, while the skylight brings in natural light and makes the bathroom feel larger and brighter throughout the day. The vanity area was upgraded with a sleek floating vanity, a wall-mounted faucet, and an LED backlit round mirror, delivering a sharp, custom look that’s both functional and timeless.

If you’re searching for Washington, DC bathroom remodeling or a premium DMV bathroom renovation, this project shows how the right materials and precise installation can turn an ordinary bathroom into a true spa experience.
