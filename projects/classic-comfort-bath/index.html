Rapido Bathrooms – Modern Bathroom Remodel in Springfield, Virginia

Rapido Bathrooms just completed this stunning modern bathroom renovation in Springfield, VA, blending clean design, high-end finishes, and practical luxury. This project showcases our signature fast turnaround, expert craftsmanship, and fully permitted remodeling process in Northern Virginia.

Custom Walk-In Glass Shower

A spacious, frameless glass shower creates an open, luxury spa feel. Featuring dual matte black rainfall shower heads, sleek wall controls, and a custom tiled base with a center drain, this shower is built for both style and performance. The marble threshold adds a premium finish while keeping the design clean and modern.

Statement Tile Work

The bathroom features bold black hexagon floor tile that grounds the space with a contemporary edge. Inside the shower, we used a vertical linear tile paired with a textured accent wall for visual depth and contrast.

Freestanding Soaking Tub

Positioned near the large window, the modern freestanding bathtub turns this space into a relaxing retreat. Natural light enhances the open layout while highlighting the minimalist tub design and matte black floor-mounted filler.

Custom Double Vanity

A sleek floating-style wood vanity with a warm natural finish adds balance to the cool tile tones. The double sink setup, paired with matte black faucets and framed mirrors, delivers both functionality and modern elegance.
