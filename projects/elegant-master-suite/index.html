Washington, DC Bathroom Remodel — Clean Modern Upgrade with Frameless Shower

This Washington, DC bathroom remodel delivers a bright, modern refresh with clean lines, durable finishes, and a layout designed for everyday comfort. The space was upgraded with crisp, light-toned marble-look tile and a minimalist palette that makes the bathroom feel larger, fresher, and more open—an ideal fit for homeowners searching for bathroom remodeling in Washington DC and high-quality DC bathroom renovation.

The new shower features a sleek frameless glass enclosure, a modern linear-style drain look, and two recessed niches for clutter-free storage. Large-format wall tile keeps grout lines minimal for a cleaner appearance and easier maintenance, while the contrasting dark shower floor tile adds depth and a premium finish.
