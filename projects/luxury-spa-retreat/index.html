Modern Spa Bathroom Remodel (Freestanding Tub + Frameless Glass Shower)

A bright, modern spa-style bathroom with a clean gray-and-white palette, bold black accents, and a layout built for comfort and everyday function. Large-format marble-look wall tile keeps the room crisp and timeless, while the soft gray floor tile adds warmth and durability. The result is an airy, high-end bathroom that feels both minimal and luxurious—designed for easy maintenance, long-term performance, and a polished modern look.

Freestanding Soaking Tub + Window Feature

A modern freestanding bathtub anchors the space and creates a relaxing focal point. The floor-mounted matte black tub filler adds a sharp, contemporary edge, while the large window brings in natural light that enhances the clean, bright finishes.

Modern Double Vanity & Clean Lines

A long double vanity brings function for busy mornings with generous storage and a bright countertop finish. Matte black fixtures, rectangular mirrors, and streamlined lighting keep everything sharp and contemporary without feeling cold.

Smart Storage + Finishing Touches

Floating shelving and simple accessories make the room feel styled but practical—ideal for towels, decor, and everyday essentials without clutter.

Frameless Walk-In Shower with Designer Details

The glass walk-in shower keeps sightlines open and adds a premium look. Inside, the shower features a bold rainfall head, a clean wall-mounted hand shower, and a built-in niche for organized storage. The dark hex mosaic shower floor adds contrast and traction while tying into the modern palette.
